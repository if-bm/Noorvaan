Candle Care

  • Trim wick to ~1/4” before each burn.
  • First burn: allow a full melt pool (2–3 hours) to prevent tunneling.
  • Burn on a heat-resistant surface, away from drafts and pets.
  • Do not burn more than 4 hours at a time.
  • Stop burning when ~1/2” of wax remains.

Safety first — never leave a burning candle unattended.
