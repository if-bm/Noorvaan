Our Story

Noorvaan means “carrier of light.” Our candles draw from South Asian heritage and timeless rituals, crafted for modern spaces.

We partner with trusted suppliers for clean wax and fine fragrance oils. Each batch is hand-poured with care to ensure clean, consistent burns.

Materials

  • Premium wax blend for a clean, even burn
  • Cotton wicks — lead and zinc free
  • Fine fragrance oils, thoughtfully balanced

Sustainability

We favor recyclable packaging and encourage jar reuse. Our goal is to minimize waste while maximizing delight.
